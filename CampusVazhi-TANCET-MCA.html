ABOUT THE EXAM

What is TANCET MCA?

TANCET MCA is the MCA-specific paper of the Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test, conducted by Anna University. It's the sole entrance exam for MCA program admissions across 50+ colleges in Tamil Nadu. The MCA paper differs from TANCET MBA — it has a Computer Awareness section instead of Data Interpretation.

MCA from TN government colleges offers 3-year CS-focused education at subsidized fees. Colleges like PSG Tech, Coimbatore Institute, and Anna University main campus MCA programs are sought-after. The exam pattern is straightforward with good scope for thorough preparation.

FAQS

TANCET MCA — Frequently Asked Questions

How is TANCET MCA different from TANCET Management?
TANCET MCA has Computer Awareness (25Q) instead of DI/GK. The rest (Quant, Reasoning, English) overlaps. MCA paper tests CS fundamentals like programming, data structures, and OS.
What is the syllabus for Computer Awareness in TANCET MCA?
Programming concepts (C/C++/Java), Data Structures (arrays, stacks, queues, trees), Algorithms (sorting, searching), Operating Systems, DBMS basics, and Computer Networks fundamentals.
What MCA colleges can I get with TANCET MCA?
Admission to 50+ colleges: PSG Tech (Coimbatore), GCT, Thiagarajar, MIT, KCET, government colleges (admission fee: ₹5,000-15,000/year). Private colleges: ₹30,000-60,000/year.
Is NIMCET better than TANCET MCA?
NIMCET gets you NIT-level MCA, which is superior in terms of brand and placements. TANCET MCA covers TN colleges which are strong regionally. If you can crack NIMCET, prioritize it.
What is the exam date for TANCET MCA 2026?
TANCET 2026 (both Management and MCA papers) is scheduled for May 9, 2026.
