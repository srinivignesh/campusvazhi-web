How to Prepare

A structured month-by-month roadmap to crack this exam

1
Understand the Exam Pattern (Week 1)
Group 1 has 3 stages: Preliminary (200 MCQ, 300 marks), Mains (6 descriptive papers), and Interview (120 marks). Know the weightage before you start.
2
Build a 12-Month Foundation Plan
Months 1–4: Cover GS basics (History, Polity, Geography, Economy). Months 5–8: Tamil Nadu specifics + Science & Tech. Months 9–12: Current affairs daily + mock tests.
3
Master General Tamil / General English
Paper II in Mains (250 marks each) is qualifying. Start Tamil grammar and comprehension from Month 1 — do not leave it for last.
4
Current Affairs — Daily Without Fail
Read The Hindu + PIB + Dinamalar. Group 1 questions are heavily current-affairs-linked in both Prelims and Mains.
5
Answer Writing Practice
Mains is descriptive. Practice 10-mark and 15-mark answers weekly from Month 6. Speed + structure = your competitive edge.
6
Mock Tests & Previous Papers
Attempt at least 40 full-length Prelims mocks and write 5 full Mains mock exams. Analyse every mistake — don't just count scores.
