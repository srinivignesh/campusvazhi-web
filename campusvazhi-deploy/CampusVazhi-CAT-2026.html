7-Month Study Plans

CAT prep isn't one-size-fits-all. Pick the plan that matches your reality.

College Student

The Fresher Plan

4 hrs/day · Weekends intensive · 28 weeks
Months 1-2
  • QA fundamentals (30 topics)
  • Daily RC (2 passages)
  • LR puzzles foundation
Months 3-5
  • Sectional tests weekly
  • Advanced DILR sets
  • VARC vocabulary sprints
Months 6-7
  • 20 full mocks + analysis
  • Weakness fixing cycles
  • Exam day strategy drill
Working Professional

The 9-to-9 Plan

2 hrs weekday · 5 hrs weekend · 28 weeks
Weekday Routine
  • 30 min RC on commute
  • 1 hr QA concept after dinner
  • 15 min GK/news scroll
Weekend Block
  • Saturday: full sectional test
  • Sunday: analysis + concept patch
  • Every 3rd weekend: full mock
Reality Checks
  • Sick week? Skip mocks, not concepts
  • Month 6: apply for exam leave
  • Protect sleep at all costs
Second Attempt

The Repeater Plan

3 hrs/day · Diagnostic-driven · 20 weeks
Diagnostic Phase
  • Audit last year's mock trail
  • Identify 3 killer weaknesses
  • Reset plan around gaps
Repair Phase
  • Concept rebuilds for weak areas
  • Mock every 10 days
  • Attempt strategy revamp
Peak Phase
  • Simulated exam-day mocks
  • Stamina & time tradeoffs
  • Mindset reset: new attempt, not redo
