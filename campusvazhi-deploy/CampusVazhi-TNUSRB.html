How to Prepare

A structured month-by-month roadmap to crack this exam

1
Know Your Post & Eligibility
Constable/Grade II PC: 10th pass + physical standards. Sub-Inspector of Police: Degree required. Jail Warden/Fireman: 12th pass. Each post has different height/weight/chest norms for male and female candidates.
2
Start Physical Training Immediately
Physical Test is eliminatory and comes before the written exam for some posts. Begin running (1.5 km in 7 min for men), long jump, and endurance drills from Day 1. Don't underestimate this stage.
3
Written Exam — General Knowledge
The written test covers: General Knowledge, Science, History, Geography, Current Affairs, Tamil Nadu Affairs, and Aptitude. Aim for 70%+ in written to compensate for physical variation.
4
Tamil Language & Reasoning
Tamil paper is a key differentiator. Revise grammar (எழுத்து, சொல், பொருள்), comprehension, and proverbs. Reasoning section rewards daily 30-minute practice.
5
Previous Papers & Mock Tests
TNUSRB follows a predictable pattern. Solve last 5 years' question papers. Tamil Nadu-specific GK (district HQs, rivers, government schemes) appears every year.
6
Medical & Document Readiness
Ensure your vision, height, weight, and chest measurements meet the notification standards. Gather all certificates (nativity, community, education) early — shortfalls at verification can disqualify you after all the hard work.
