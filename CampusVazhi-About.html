Our Mission

We make the picture clear.
You decide what's best for you.

Every year, thousands of Tamil Nadu students make life-altering decisions about college and careers with incomplete information — or worse, information twisted by consultants with a financial stake in the outcome. CampusVazhi was built to fix that. We gather everything you need, lay it out plainly in Tamil and English, and leave the decision entirely with you.

Zero Commissions
We don't earn referral fees from colleges, coaching centres, or consultancies. Our advice is never influenced by what earns us money.
Transparent Information
Cutoffs, fee structures, placement records, real timelines — we show you the complete picture, including the parts that aren't flattering.
Built by Alumni Who've Been There
Our mentors are IIM graduates, experienced professionals, and university alumni who navigated the same system — not salespeople with a script.
Free. Forever.
Every resource — PYQ papers, mock tests, syllabus guides, cutoff data — is free. We believe financial barriers should never block good guidance.

Why Free?

Because access to good guidance shouldn't depend on how much you can pay.

Premium coaching and counselling services charge ₹50,000–₹2,00,000 for information that should be freely available. We believe that a student in a small town in Tamil Nadu deserves exactly the same quality of guidance as someone who can afford a top consultant in Chennai.

CampusVazhi is currently passion-funded. We plan to remain free for all students. If you find value here, the best way to support us is to share with one more student who needs it.
